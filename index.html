Share your annotation in alignment with others.

Persistent repository

Anyone can upload his/her annotation (to PubMed or the open access set of PMC articles) on PubAnnotation. The uploaders keep full control over their annotation, e.g., revision, deletion and access control.

Alignment

Annotations are aligned with other annotations, becoming comparable, collectively usable, and so on.

REST API

Annotations are accessable through REST API.

Access annotation

URL

http://pubannotation.org/projects/bionlp-st-ge-2013-training/docs/sourcedb/PMC/sourceid/2626671/divs/1 /spans/1753-1870/annotations.json
